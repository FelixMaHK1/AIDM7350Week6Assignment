VR Nursing Training - Holo Baby

This project is a VR application designed to help nursing students practice caring for a baby experiencing Febrile Convulsion in a virtual environment. The students are expected to apply the knowledge and skills learned in class, such as the "three checks five rights," monitoring vital signs, using AOM, and completing MAR documents. This application also supports multiplayer, allowing two nursing students to complete the training simultaneously.
